Main controls

  • WASD to move
  • left click to throw your line
  • right click to reel in

Chat

  • '/' to start chat
  • 'Enter' to submit
  • 'Esc' to cancel

Other controls

  • Zig zag as you run for a boost
  • 'r' to reset hooks
  • 'shift' to throw your line relative to your motion (hold it down)
  • 'c' to reset camera
